Ep #4: Vape Holdings with Kyle Tracey

In this episode we chat with Kyle Tracey the CEO of Vape Holdings (makers of Hive Ceramics with over 1000 retail locations). Kyle talks with us about his journey through the industry starting with a lucky break at the Cannabis Cup, creating and selling his own company, and eventually running the publicly traded Vape Holdings.
