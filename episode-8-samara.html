Ep #8: Samara Miranda

In this episode we chat with Samara Miranda, a former California dispensary owner who had to close up shop due to DEA threats. We chat with Samara about how she grew her dispensary to 10 employees and then had to abruptly shut down after being notified by the DEA. Samara also shares some successful guerrilla marketing techniques she used to grow and tells us her #1 tip for dispensary owners.
