Ep #6: Karing Kind with Dylan Donaldson

July 6, 2015

In this episode we chat with Dylan Donaldson, the owner of Karing Kind - the first recreational dispensary in Boulder Colorado. We chat with Dylan about how running a dispensary can be a grind, why he's passionate about selling only organic cannabis, and why he's hopeful about the future of the industry.

Ep #5: NCIA with Taylor West

June 4, 2015

In this episode we chat with Taylor West, Deputy Director at the National Cannabis Industry Association (NCIA). The NCIA is the only national trade association advancing the interests of the legitimate and responsible cannabis industry. Taylor talks with us about barriers to Federal legalization, how politicians are the least brave people on the planet, and how education is still the #1 issue to help with marijuana legalization.

Ep #4: Vape Holdings with Kyle Tracey

May 4, 2015

In this episode we chat with Kyle Tracey the CEO of Vape Holdings (makers of Hive Ceramics with over 1000 retail locations). Kyle talks with us about his journey through the industry starting with a lucky break at the Cannabis Cup, creating and selling his own company, and eventually running the publicly traded Vape Holdings.

Ep #3: Marijuana Policy Project with Morgan Fox

April 29, 2015

In this episode we chat with Morgan Fox of the Marijuana Policy Project (MPP) the largest organization working solely on marijuana reform in the United States. Morgan discusses the trends in cannabis legalization, the failures of the drug war, and even Chris Christie.

Ep #2: AMCH Denver with Dave Kulikov

April 3, 2015

In this episode we chat with Dave Kulikov of AMCH Denver, a top dispensary on Capitol Hill. Dave is the owner and shares his deep knowledge and experience about the cannabis industry, why running a dispensary is hard work, and how to prepare if you want to open up your own shop.

Ep #1: MMJ America with Shane Flanagan

March 26, 2015

In our kick off episode we interview Shane Flanagan from MMJ America, one of the top dispensaries in Colorado. Shane is a manager at their Boulder location and we chat with him about how he got into the cannabis industry, why MMJ is so successful, what the future holds for cannabis, and more!
