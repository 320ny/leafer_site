Ep #7: Olivia Mannix - Cannabrand

In this episode we chat with Olivia Mannix, part owner of Cannabrand - the world's leading cannabis marketing agency headquartered in Denver, CO. We chat with Olivia about all the marketing hurdles dispensaries and other cannabis professionals face, how the social stigma of marijuana is starting to wear off, and how crime has been decreasing thanks to legalization.
